After inherting the family sewing business from his father, Jemal Adem fell in love with all things sewing, tailoring, and embroidery. From a young age, Adem began producing vibrant cultural Ethiopian suits and wedding dresses. Despite leaving his home country behind, Adem was passionate about keeping the family sewing business alive. For the past fourteen years, Adem has been creating quality clothing pieces, both cultural and modern, for his local community.

At Adem Sewing we value creating a great customer experience and providing a high standard service founded on communcation and affordability.
